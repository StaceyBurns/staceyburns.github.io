MULTIMEDIA SHOWCASE

LIT CLONMEL


6pm - Wednesday 27nd May 2016

LSAD - Clonmel showcases the talents of its students each year with an exhibition. The exhibition allows students to celebrate their creative talents, while showcasing them to the public. Each year the exhibition is organised by Creative Multimedia students, who are responsible for promoting and organising the exhibition. In the past the event has been known as Digital Expressions, the name has been changed to Pen & Pixel. It was felt that this new name better reflected the creative process of designing and then creating a piece of work. This year a particular emphasis has been placed on showcasing both digital and analog work, spanning the entire range of the multimedia sector.
